# 🕊️ Bridges of Peace (IL & IR)

**Bridges of Peace** is a grassroots humanitarian web application created during a time of conflict to remind us of our shared history and common humanity. Designed specifically for citizens in both Israel and Iran who are spending time in shelters, this app serves as a digital “handshake” across borders that politics cannot close.

## 🌟 The Mission

In times of darkness, we find the light together. This project aims to:

- **Humanize:** Break the cycle of “us vs. them” by focusing on the real people behind the maps.
- **Educate:** Share the deep, ancient history between the Jewish and Persian peoples through interactive trivia.
- **Comfort:** Provide a peaceful atmosphere with music and AI-generated imagery that celebrates the beauty of both cultures.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Multilingual Support:** Full Right-to-Left (RTL) support for Hebrew and Farsi, as well as English.
- **Cultural Trivia:** A pool of 40+ questions (20 for each side) exploring the foods, kings, poets, and traditions that connect Israel and Persia.
- **Peace Radio:** Instant access to live music streams:
  - 🇮🇱 **Mizrahit FM:** High-energy Israeli Mediterranean hits.
  - 🇮🇷 **Radio Javan:** The world’s top Persian music station.
- **AI Art Generation:** Powered by the Gemini API, the app visualizes peace by generating high-quality cinematic art of landmarks like Shiraz and Jerusalem merging in harmony.
- **Social Solidarity:** A final sharing tool that allows users to broadcast their message of hope to social media platforms.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

- **Frontend:** Single-file HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.
- **Icons:** Lucide-React.
- **AI:** Google Gemini API (Imagen 4.0) for real-time cultural visualization.
- **Design:** Mobile-first, “Shelter Dark Mode” to save battery and reduce eye strain.

## 📦 Deployment

This app is designed to be “Plug & Play”. It requires no server-side setup or database configuration.

1. Clone the repository.
2. Open `index.html`.
3. *(Optional)* Insert your own Gemini API key in the script section to enable AI image generation.
